KOTTAYAM

CITY OF LETTERS

Kottayam is a city in the Indian state of Kerala. Kottayam literally means the interior of a fort "Kotta" meaning fort and "Akam" meaning inside. It is situated in the basin of the Meenachil River and in the basin of the Vembanad backwaters, which are formed from several streams in the Western Ghats of the Idukki district.

Places to Visit

Illikkal Kallu

Illikkal Kallu is a monolith located on top of the Illickal Malaa in the Kottayam district of Kerala, India.The distance from kottayam railway station to illikal kallu is 57km

Ilaveezhapoonchira

Ilaveezhapoonchira is a tourist destination located in Melukavu village in Kottayam district near Kanjar. Ilaveezha Poonchira is surrounded by three enchanting hillocks - Mankunnu, Kodayathoormala and Thonippara. This makes the place ideal for trekking.

Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple

Serene 16th-century Hindu temple known for its traditional Kerala-style architecture & murals. It is situated at the heart of the city.
